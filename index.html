My Mission Statement

Ever since I was in primary school, I had already gained an interest in Robotics. I was so intrigued by it that I knew that I had to get into the CCA in secondary school. During my secondary school years, I started to grow by learning from the people around me, asking around if I didn't know what to do and practising on my own. My hardwork had paid off and I was appointed the Chairperson of the Fuhua Robotics Club AY15/16. This was when I realised I fared better in and preferred programming rather than building the robot and was passionate in it, hence I decided to pursue my diploma in the IT sector.

Working towards my dream of either becoming a software engineer or a cyber security specialist, I am constantly trying to upgrade myself. Electives that I have chosen are Secure Software Development and also Data Structures & Algorithm. Which teaches us to protect websites and build better codes more efficiently.

I believe that I have attained the necessary skillsets to be able to give back to the company and excel in any role that I am placed in. I want to experience how it is like working in the real-world and step out of my comfort zone, grabbing opportunities even if they seem risky because we will never know if we never try.

ToiletLeh

Ever needed to go to the toilet so badly, but unable to find one? There have been grievances by people who find it difficult to locate a toilet, especially in areas where signs to go to the toilet is poorly placed or in tucked-away areas like HDB blocks. Furthermore, many public toilets are poorly maintained and lack essential items such as hand soap and toilet paper. Thus, we decided to create ToiletLeh – a free mobile application that shows users the nearest toilet in the vicinity, how to get there as quick as possible and also show the cleanliness rating of the toilet.
