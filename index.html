ToiletLeh

Ever needed to go to the toilet so badly, but unable to find one? There have been grievances by people who find it difficult to locate a toilet, especially in areas where signs to go to the toilet is poorly placed or in tucked-away areas like HDB blocks. Furthermore, many public toilets are poorly maintained and lack essential items such as hand soap and toilet paper. Thus, we decided to create ToiletLeh – a free mobile application that shows users the nearest toilet in the vicinity, how to get there as quick as possible and also show the cleanliness rating of the toilet.

Python, Kivy, Raspberry Pi 3
